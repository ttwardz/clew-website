Clew 1.0 Launch

April 7, 2025

After 12 TestFlight builds and 4 years of development, Clew is live on the App Store and available to download for free.

"Clew was born from a simple idea—what if your highlights, thoughts, and margin notes could follow you anywhere, across all your books and media, rather than be trapped in individual apps and platforms?"

Thank you to all the beta testers who helped make this possible! Your feedback and suggestions have been instrumental in creating version 1.0 and directing where Clew will go in the future.
