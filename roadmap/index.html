Coming Soon to Clew

Here's a list of features in the works for upcoming updates:

  • Even faster highlight creation, with better book, website, and tag autocomplete and suggestions.
  • Save highlights directly from a wider variety of apps, like Books, Kindle, Serial Reader, and more.
  • More source types: in addition to books and webpages, you'll be able to add movies, TV shows, podcasts, and more.
  • Use the Notes field to link to other books, authors, and highlights.
  • Create and manage custom Collections.
  • Lots more...
