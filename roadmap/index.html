Here's a list of features coming soon to Clew:

  • Faster highlight creation, with better book and tag suggestions.
  • Save highlights directly from other apps, like Safari, Books, Kindle, Serial Reader, and more using the Share Sheet.
  • More source types: in addition to books, you'll be able to add movies, TV shows, podcasts, and more.
  • Use the Notes field to link to other books, authors, and highlights.
  • Create and manage custom Collections.
