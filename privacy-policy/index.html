tl;dr

Neither the developers ("We") of Clew nor the app or its website collects, stores, or shares any of your personal information.

Full Privacy Policy

We don't gather or share any information about you as a user of our app, Clew, or its website ("Services"). When you use our Services, your data is private to you.

If you have allowed Apple to collect information from your device and share it with developers, we may receive some limited and anonymous analytics information.

There is no tracking technology in Clew.

Your Clew data is stored in your personal iCloud Drive database, which means it exists within your Apple account, on your device(s), and nowhere else.

Any machine learning (ML)-powered capabilities, such as intelligent tag suggestion, uses on-device frameworks. No information leaves your device, and no data is sent to or received from the internet.

For information about the data sources Clew uses, see the "Data Sources" section of this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at help@getclew.com.

If you send us an email at help@getclew.com, we will see the email address you used to communicate with us along with any personal information you might include, such as your name or an email signature. We will not share this information with anyone.

We are committed to privacy, trust, and your ownership of your data.

External Data Sources

Book information: We use the Google Books API to autofill book information, including cover images, from the internet when you choose to do so in the Clew app. This is a get-only request. None of your information is shared.
